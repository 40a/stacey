title: About Anthony

content: 
Ante is a graphic designer from Melbourne, Australia. 
He graduated from Swinburne University in 2006 with a Bachelor of Design - Honours. This was where he first discovered the writings of Bob Gill, Alan Fletcher and the work of the conceptual artists such as Joseph Kosuth and Hans Haacke. Their ideologies had a considerable effect on his approach to graphic design. He now works professionally as a senior designer.
